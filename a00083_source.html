72  /// Selects a random triangle in the set P (then we add another point to keep the
73  /// base as planar as possible). We apply a simple heuristic that works in most
74  /// practical cases. The idea is to accept maximum distance, computed by the
75  /// estimated overlap, multiplied by the diameter of P, and try to have
76  /// a triangle with all three edges close to this distance. Wide triangles helps
77  /// to make the transformation robust while too large triangles makes the
78  /// probability of having all points in the inliers small so we try to trade-off.
79  inline bool SelectQuadrilateral(Scalar &invariant1, Scalar &invariant2,
80  int& base1, int& base2, int& base3, int& base4);
